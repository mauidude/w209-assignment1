Money Spent at Amazon in 2019

This dataset is an aggregation of the money I spent at Amazon in 2019 by day.

Some insights:

  • Saturdays appear to be the day when most purchases are made
  • Spending increases toward the end of the year around the holidays
  • There's an uptick in purchases the first week of September due to moving to a new house on 8/31
  • Black Friday appears to be one of the highest spending days
